HOW TO USE KHATABOOK

Khatabook helps you manage companies, people, and transactions easily.
You can tap to open items and long press to edit or delete them.

---

COMPANY MANAGEMENT

Create a Company

1. Open the Companies section. (top right icon)
2. Tap Add Company.(top right icon)
3. Enter the company name and optional notes.
4. Tap Save.

Edit / Modify a Company
You can edit a company in two ways.

Method 1: Long Press

1. Long press on the company name in the company list.
2. The Update Company screen will open.
3. Edit the details and tap Save.



Delete a Company

1. Long press on the company name.
2. Tap Delete Company.
3. Confirm deletion.

Note: Deleting a company will permanently remove all people and transactions under it.

---

PERSON (CUSTOMER / PARTY) MANAGEMENT

Create a Person

1. Open a company.
2. Go to the People section. ("+" symbol at bottom right, inside a company).

3. Tap Add Person.
4. Enter the person name, phone number (optional).
5. Tap Save.

Edit / Modify a Person
You can edit a person in two ways.

Method 1: Long Press

1. Long press on the person name in the people list.
2. The Update Person screen will open.
3. Edit the details and tap Save.


Delete a Person

1. Long press on the person name.
2. Tap Delete Person.
3. Confirm deletion.

Note: All transactions related to this person will be deleted.

---

TRANSACTIONS

1. Open a person profile.
2. Tap Add Transaction.
3. Choose You Gave or You Got.
4. Enter the amount, date, and notes.
5. Tap Save.

---

EXPORT AND SHARE DATA (CSV)

Download / Share Company CSV

1. Open a company.
2. Tap Export or Download CSV.
3. Choose Share or Save.
4. The CSV file will include company details, people, and balances.

Download / Share Person CSV

1. Open a person profile.
2. Tap Export or Download CSV.
3. Choose Share or Save.
4. The CSV file will include person details, transactions, and balance summary.

---

BACKUP AND DATA SAFETY

All data is stored locally on your device.
Exporting CSV files helps you keep manual backups.
You can save CSV files to Google Drive or any cloud storage.

---

DATA PRIVACY

Your data stays on your device.
Files are shared only when you choose to share them.

---

QUICK GESTURE GUIDE

Tap on a company or person to open details.
Long press on a company or person to edit or delete.
